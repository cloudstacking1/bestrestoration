What Is water damage restoration service?

Water damage restoration is a professional service that involves cleaning, repairing, and restoring properties that have been affected by water-related incidents such as flooding, burst pipes, leaks, sewage backups, or storms.

Key Features:

  • Assessment and Inspection:

    • The restoration team will first inspect the property to determine the extent of the water damage, identify the source of the water (clean, gray, or black water), and create a restoration plan.

  • Water Removal (Extraction):

    • Using industrial-grade pumps and vacuums, the technicians will quickly extract standing water from floors, carpets, and other affected areas. Fast water removal is crucial to prevent further damage and mold growth.

  • Drying and Dehumidifying:

    • After the bulk of the water is removed, high-powered air movers and dehumidifiers are used to dry out remaining moisture from walls, floors, and furniture. Hidden moisture can cause long-term structural issues and mold.

  • Cleaning and Sanitizing:

    • All affected items and surfaces are thoroughly cleaned and disinfected. This includes antimicrobial treatments to prevent bacteria and mold. Personal belongings like furniture, clothing, and documents may also be restored if salvageable.
